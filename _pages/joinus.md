## Graduate students
Our Lab can recruit Masters and PhD students through the [Department of Marine and Coastal Environmental Science](https://marine.tamu.edu/academics/marine-coastal-environmental-science/index.html) at Texas A&M University at Galveston or PhD students through the [Department of Oceanography](https://artsci.tamu.edu/oceanography/index.html) (College Station) at Texas A&M University. 

Preference is given to students who has 
* good mathmatic backgournd
* programming skill in Python
* prior experience with ocean model running in Linux environment
* the courage to explore new direction independently and collabratively

If you are interested in joining the lab, please contact me at jdu@tamug.edu a couple of months before the application deadline (often January 1st) with the following materials:

* CV
* Unofficial academic transcript

## Undergraduate students
