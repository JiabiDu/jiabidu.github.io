### Desired Qualifications:
We are looking for motivated students who possess:
* A strong **mathematical background**.
* Proficiency in **programming** (preferably Python).
* Prior experience with **numerical ocean models** in a Linux environment.
* The **initiative** to explore new research directions both independently and collaboratively.

### How to Apply:
If you are interested in joining the lab, please contact Dr. Du at **jdu@tamug.edu** several months before the application deadline (typically January 1st). In your email, please include the following materials:
* A current **CV**.
* **Unofficial transcripts**.
* A brief statement of your research interests and why you want to join our lab.
