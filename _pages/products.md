## Oyster larvae tracker
Info: this is a web-based dashboard application that allows users to click any location in Galveston Bay to check the movement of passive particle (wihtout any buoyancy/settling behavior) over 30 days.

Dashboard application is available at:
[https://coastalmodeling.org/ptrack/](https://coastalmodeling.org/ptrack/)
![larvae tracker](/images/larvae_tracker.png)

## Texas forecast hydrodynamic model
Info: this hydrodynamic model, originally developed by our lab, is now officially adopted as the operational model by Texas Water Development Board. 

![model grid](/images/run270b_model_grid.png)

The hindcast and forecast products are available at [https://midgewater.twdb.texas.gov](https://midgewater.twdb.texas.gov)
