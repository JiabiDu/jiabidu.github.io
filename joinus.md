## Graduate students
Our Lab can recruit Masters and PhD students through the Department of Marine and Coastal Environmental Science at Texas A&M University at Galveston or PhD students through the Department of Oceanography (College Station) at Texas A&M University. 

If you are interested in joining the lab, I recommend reaching out to me at jdu@tamug.edu a couple of months before the application deadline January 1st with the following materials:

* CV
* Unofficial academic transcript

## Undergraduate students
If you are interested in joining the lab, reach me any time at jdu@tamug.edu.
